Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

Scissa built the Scissa app as a commercial/utility desktop application. This service is provided by Scissa and is intended for use as is.

1. Information Collection & Use

Scissa operates entirely locally on your device. We **do not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, video files, metadata, or path settings with any remote servers or third parties. All video processing (probing and splitting) is executed offline on your local CPU using FFmpeg.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

This application uses local device configurations (JSON settings files and HTML5 local storage) strictly to persist your preferences, such as your FFmpeg binary path, default output folders, and whether Advanced Mode is enabled. None of this information is transmitted online.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertisements, tracking SDKs, or cloud storage systems. The app runs completely sandboxed and offline.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.
