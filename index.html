Split videos in 3 simple steps

01

Import Video

Drag and drop your video file. Scissa automatically probes metadata like codec, resolution, and length.

02

Set Split Targets

Choose whether to split by target segment size (e.g., 80MB Discord limit) or target duration (e.g., 5-minute chunks).

03

Start Splitting

Hit "Start Split" and watch the real-time progress update. Open the output directory to get your segments!
